About this project

Wikibase is the software that drives Wikidata, as well as countless other sites across the Internet -- from arts organizations to archives, museums and many more. Here's a list of case studies and installations.

Why Wikibase?

Wikibase allows an organization to manage structured data accessibly and in a language-independent way both humans and machines can understand. Its powerful tooling allows for detailed searches using SPARQL and for bulk import and management of your data.

How do I use Wikibase?

We're so glad you asked! You can run an example query against the formidable Wikibase instance that is Wikidata, jump into our Docker quick start, or take a look at our comprehensive resource documentation.
